Who We Are?

A caring community believers committed to raising disciples of Jesus Christ.

How We Started?

The Filipino Christian Fellowship (FCF), also known as "New Horizons Community," is a supportive Christian community for Filipinos in Toronto and beyond. Established in 2012 and formally becoming a congregation in 2015, FCF aims to create a spiritually uplifting environment that respects the cultural and spiritual experiences of Filipino immigrants. The church offers worship services, care groups, and outreach programs focused on strengthening families and relationships. As a mainline Evangelical church, its mission is to share God's love through Christ by loving God, loving people, and making disciples. The fellowship welcomes all to join in its journey of faith and community.

Regular Service

every Sunday, 10:00 am

Worship at FCF is spirit-filled and “blended,” a combination of contemporary and classic hymns and songs at 10:00 a.m. Messages (sermons) are based on a passage of the Bible with a focus on life application.

Faith Groups

Faith Groups (from children to adults) meet after the worship service and other days of week for Bible study, relationship and prayer.

Get Together

Fellowship follows after the faith group meeting for a shared meal to encourage one another.
